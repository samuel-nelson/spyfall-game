SPYFALL

CLASSIFIED OPERATION

HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Non-spy agents must identify the spy through strategic questioning. The spy must identify the location or survive until time expires.

SETUP: Each player receives a location card—except one player, who is the spy and receives no location.

GAMEPLAY: Players take turns asking and answering questions about the location. The spy must blend in without revealing ignorance of the location.

WINNING: Non-spies win by correctly identifying the spy through majority vote. The spy wins by guessing the location or surviving until time runs out.
