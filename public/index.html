HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Non-mole agents must identify the mole through strategic questioning. The mole must identify the location or survive until time expires.

SETUP: Each player receives a location card—except one player, who is the mole and receives no location.

GAMEPLAY: Players take turns asking and answering questions about the location. The mole must blend in without revealing ignorance of the location.

WINNING: Non-moles win by correctly identifying the mole through majority vote. The mole wins by guessing the location or surviving until time runs out.
